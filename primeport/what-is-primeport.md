# What is Primeport

PrimePort is the **first and largest NFT marketplace on XDC Network**.

It enables the creation, buying, selling, and auctioning of NFTs across **EVM-compatible chains**, while also serving as the primary secondary market for **XDC NFTs** and **PRFI NFTs** generated by PrimeStaking.

***

### Overview

PrimePort combines standard NFT marketplace functionality with a core role inside the Prime Numbers ecosystem:

* NFT trading infrastructure across EVM chains
* Secondary market for staking positions
* Liquidity layer for PrimeStaking

***

### Market Position

* Live since **2022**
* The **leading NFT marketplace on XDC** by volume
