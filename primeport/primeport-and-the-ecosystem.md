# Primeport & The Ecosystem

## PrimePort and the Ecosystem

PrimePort plays two roles in the Prime Numbers ecosystem simultaneously:

* A **standalone marketplace**
* The **liquidity layer for staking positions**

***

### PrimePort → PrimeStaking

The secondary market for **XDC NFTs** and **PRFI NFTs** introduces:

* Real price discovery for staking positions
* Immediate exit liquidity

A staker who wants to exit does not need to wait for the unbonding period.\
They can sell their NFT vault on PrimePort at market price.

***

### PrimePort → $PRFI

PrimePort charges a **1.5% fee** on all secondary sales.

That fee is distributed as follows:

* 50% → Company & Treasury
* 16.66% → XDC NFT holders
* 33.34% → PRFI NFT holders

Every trade on PrimePort generates **direct revenue for $PRFI stakers**.

***

### PrimePort → PrimeFi (Upcoming)

Phase 1 of NFT integration within PrimeFi is targeted for **Q1–Q2 2026**.

This will enable:

* Ecosystem NFTs to be used as collateral
* NFT-based lending positions within PrimeFi
