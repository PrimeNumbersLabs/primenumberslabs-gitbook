# What is Primeport

PrimePort is the first and largest NFT marketplace on XDC Network. It supports\
the creation, buying, selling, and auctioning of NFTs across EVM chains,\
and serves as the primary secondary market for XDC NFTs and PRFI NFTs\
generated by PrimeStaking.

Live since 2022. The leading NFT marketplace on XDC by volume.
