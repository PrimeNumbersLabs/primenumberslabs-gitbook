# What is PrimeFi

PrimeFi is an omnichain lending and borrowing protocol, the first of its kind\
to let users deposit collateral on one chain and borrow on another, natively,\
without wrapped tokens or centralized bridges.

Built on LayerZero's cross-chain messaging infrastructure and integrated with\
Stargate Finance for liquidity, PrimeFi operates across XDC Network,\
Hyperliquid, and Base, with Ethereum and Arbitrum on the expansion roadmap.

$4M+ in active borrows. Audited by PeckShield, QuillAudits, and\
pre-audited by Gerard Persoon (Code4rena). Live on mainnet since Q3 2025.
