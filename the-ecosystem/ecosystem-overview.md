# Ecosystem Overview

The Prime Numbers ecosystem is built around three protocols that reinforce each other.\
No single protocol operates in isolation, fees, liquidity, and rewards flow between\
all three and back to $PRFI holders.

#### **PrimeFi: Omnichain Lending & Borrowing**

Users deposit collateral and borrow across chains. A share of borrowing fees flows\
to PRFI NFT holders. Available on XDC, Hyperliquid, and Base.\
→ Full documentation: docs.primefi.xyz

#### PrimeStaking: Liquid Staking & NFT Vaults

Users stake XDC and receive psXDC 1:1, keeping their assets liquid. Staking\
positions are held inside NFTs, making them transferable. A share of staking\
rewards flows to PRFI NFT holders.\
→ Full documentation: docs.primestaking.xyz

#### PrimePort: NFT Marketplace

Users create, buy, sell, and auction NFTs across EVM chains. A share of\
marketplace fees (1.5% on secondary sales) flows to PRFI NFT holders.\
→ Full documentation: PrimePort Docs

#### $PRFI: The Ecosystem Token

$PRFI is the utility and governance token that ties all three protocols together.\
Holders who stake $PRFI inside PRFI NFTs receive a share of revenue from all\
three protocols. More detail in the $PRFI Token section.
