# PrimeStaking & The Ecosystem

## PrimeStaking

PrimeStaking is the protocol that introduced the **NFT vault mechanic**, now used across the entire Prime Numbers ecosystem.

Its connections to the other protocols are **direct and functional**, not just narrative.

***

### Protocol Integrations

#### PrimeStaking → PrimeFi

`psXDC` can be used as collateral inside **PrimeFi**.

This allows stakers to:

* Earn staking yield
* Access liquidity simultaneously

No need to choose between the two.

***

#### PrimeStaking → PrimePort

**XDC NFTs** and **PRFI NFTs** are tradeable on **PrimePort**.

This creates:

* A secondary market for staking positions
* Real price discovery for:
  * Staked XDC
  * PRFI vaults

***

#### PrimeStaking → $PRFI

A portion of PrimeStaking rewards flows to **PRFI NFT holders**.

As the protocol grows:

* TVL increases
* Revenue flowing to $PRFI stakers also increases

***
