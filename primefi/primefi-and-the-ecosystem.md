# PrimeFi & The Ecosystem

PrimeFi is the primary fee-generating protocol in the Prime Numbers ecosystem. Its connection to $PRFI and the broader ecosystem works on three levels:

Revenue sharing A share of PrimeFi borrowing fees flows directly to PRFI NFT holders. The exact split is governed by DAO vote and maintained in the Ecosystem Fees section.

$PRFI utility inside PrimeFi $PRFI holders who lock their tokens gain access to:

* Governance votes on LTV ratios, new chains, and risk parameters (soon)
* Additional $PRFI emissions for active supply and borrow participation
* Tiered analytics and portfolio health tools
* Institutional-grade risk simulations

NFT integration (upcoming) Phase 1 of NFT utility within PrimeFi is targeted for Q2-Q3 2026, enabling ecosystem NFTs to interact directly with lending positions.
