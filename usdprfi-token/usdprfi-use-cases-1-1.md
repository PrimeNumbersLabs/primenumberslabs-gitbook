# $PRFI Use Cases

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/TOKENUSECASES (1).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

$PRFI has four primary use cases within the Prime Numbers ecosystem:

1. Stake-to-Earn via NFT Vaults Lock $PRFI inside a PRFI NFT to receive a share of revenue from PrimeFi, PrimeStaking, and PrimePort. Higher tier NFTs receive a larger share. See NFT Vault System for full detail.
2. DAO Governance $PRFI is the voting token for all on-chain proposals: fee adjustments, new asset listings on PrimeFi, treasury spending, and ecosystem grants. Voting follows a Snapshot → Timelock flow. The more $PRFI you hold, the more weight your vote carries.
3. PrimeFi Protocol Utility $PRFI holders who lock their tokens unlock additional benefits within PrimeFi:

* Vote on LTV ratios, new chain deployments, and risk parameters
* Earn additional $PRFI emissions for supplying and borrowing
* Access tiered analytics: risk simulators, portfolio health alerts, and wallet dashboards
* Access institutional-grade portfolio simulations

4. Omnichain Transport As a LayerZero OFT, $PRFI bridges natively across chains — no wrapping, no liquidity pools, no third-party bridge risk. One unified supply across Ethereum, Base, XDC, Hyperliquid, and Arbitrum. Combined with Stargate Finance, users can also bridge USDC, USDT, ETH, and BTC alongside $PRFI in a single transaction.

Future Use Cases

* Collateral inside PrimeFi (pending risk parameter approval)
* Gas sponsorship for ERC-4337 smart wallets (in development)
