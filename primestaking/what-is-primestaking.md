# What is PrimeStaking

PrimeStaking is a **non-custodial liquid staking protocol** built on the **XDC Network**.

It solves the core problem that led to the creation of Prime Numbers:

> Native XDC staking requires **10,000,000 XDC** (\~$320,000) to run a masternode, along with a **1-month unbonding period**.

***

### The Solution

PrimeStaking makes XDC staking:

* Accessible to **any capital size**
* Free of **minimum requirements**
* Without **lockup periods**
* Fully **liquid at all times**

***
