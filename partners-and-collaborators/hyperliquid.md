# Hyperliquid

### Overview

**Hyperliquid** is a decentralized perpetual exchange built on **HyperEVM,** a custom Layer 1 capable of processing up to:

* 200,000 transactions per second
* 0.2 second latency

***

### Integration with Prime Numbers

Prime Numbers integrates with Hyperliquid across two key layers:

***

#### 1. PrimeFi on HyperEVM

PrimeFi is live on **Hyperliquid’s HyperEVM**, making it one of the first DeFi lending protocols deployed on the network.

This enables users to:

* Deposit collateral
* Borrow assets directly on Hyperliquid

***

#### 2. $PRFI on Hyperliquid L1

Prime Numbers secured the exclusive **$PRFI ticker** on Hyperliquid L1.

* Strategic investment: $180,000
* Ensures brand protection and exclusivity within the ecosystem

This integration enables:

* Native $PRFI presence on Hyperliquid L1 via **HIP-1**
* Connection with its OFT implementation on HyperEVM

***

### Role in the Ecosystem

The Hyperliquid integration expands Prime Numbers into:

* High-performance trading environments
* New liquidity venues
* Cross-layer asset interoperability
