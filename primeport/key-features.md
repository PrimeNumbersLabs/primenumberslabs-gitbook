# Key Features

Standard NFT Marketplace\
Create, list, buy, and sell ERC-721 NFTs across supported EVM chains.\
PrimePort supports fixed-price listings, offers, and timed auctions with\
automatic settlement.

Staking NFT Secondary Market\
XDC NFTs and PRFI NFTs, the vault positions generated by PrimeStaking,\
are tradeable on PrimePort. This creates a live secondary market for staked\
positions, allowing holders to exit their stake without going through the\
standard unbonding process.

PrimePort Drops\
Projects on XDC Network can launch NFT collections through PrimePort Drops.\
A 5% fee on all Drops projects goes to the protocol treasury.

Web3 Domains\
PrimePort supports the minting and trading of Web3 domains on XDC Network,\
human-readable addresses tied to wallet addresses.

Multichain Support\
PrimePort operates across XDC and other supported EVM chains, with\
Omnichain NFT (ONFT) standard integration via LayerZero in development\
for Q1–Q2 2026.
