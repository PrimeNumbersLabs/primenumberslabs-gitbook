# NFTs Vaults & Staking Tiers

## PrimeStaking Vault System

PrimeStaking uses **NFT vaults** as the primary staking interface, the same mechanism that powers the **$PRFI ecosystem revenue system**.

### - Types of NFT Vaults

There are two types of NFT vaults in PrimeStaking:

#### 1️⃣ XDC NFTs

Stake `psXDC` inside an **XDC NFT** to earn **XDC rewards from masternodes**.

The NFT represents your staking position and makes it fully transferable.

**Key Benefits**

* Your stake becomes a **liquid asset**
* You can **sell or transfer** your position anytime
* No need to unstake before exiting
* Trade directly on **PrimePort**

***

#### 2️⃣ PRFI NFTs

Stake `$PRFI` inside a **PRFI NFT** to earn rewards from across the entire ecosystem:

* PrimeFi fees
* PrimePort fees
* PrimeStaking yield

***

### Tier System (PRFI NFTs)

PRFI NFTs use a **tier-based reward system**:

* Staking more `$PRFI` → increases your tier
* Higher tiers → receive a larger share of ecosystem revenue

***

### Why NFTs as Vaults?

Traditional staking systems:

* Lock assets to a wallet
* Create **illiquid, non-transferable positions**

PrimeStaking changes this by using NFTs.

#### With NFT Vaults:

* Staking positions become **tradable assets**
* They can be:
  * Priced
  * Transferred
  * Sold
  * Used as collateral

All **without losing yield generation**.

***

### Core Innovation

This NFT-based staking model was the **original innovation** that started the entire **Prime Numbers ecosystem**.
