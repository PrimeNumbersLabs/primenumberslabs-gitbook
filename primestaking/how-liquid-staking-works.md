# How Liquid Staking Works

## psXDC: Liquid Staking Token

When you stake XDC through PrimeStaking, you receive **psXDC** in return, a liquid derivative token that represents your staked position at a **1:1 ratio**.

***

### How It Works

psXDC mirrors your staked XDC while remaining fully liquid.

XDC → stake → receive psXDC (1:1)

***

### What You Can Do with psXDC

psXDC is not locked. You can:

* Hold it and accumulate staking yield
* Use it as collateral inside PrimeFi
* Trade it on PrimePort
* Stake it inside an NFT vault for additional PRFI ecosystem rewards

***

### Unstaking

When you want to exit your position:

* Return your psXDC
* Receive your XDC back
* Plus any accrued yield

The unstaking process follows the **standard XDC Network unbonding period**.

***

### Under the Hood

PrimeStaking aggregates user deposits and delegates them to **XDC Network masternodes**.

This allows the protocol to:

* Capture the full validator APY (currently \~10%)
* Distribute rewards proportionally to all psXDC holders

This distribution is independent of the initial deposit size, ensuring equal access to staking yield for all participants.
