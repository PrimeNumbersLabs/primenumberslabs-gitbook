# What is Prime Numbers



Prime Numbers is a **decentralized finance ecosystem** built on XDC Network and expanded across:

* Hyperliquid
* Base
* Ethereum

It consists of three live, audited protocols **PrimeFi, PrimeStaking, and PrimePort,** unified by the **$PRFI token**.

***

### <mark style="color:purple;">Core Architecture</mark>

Each protocol serves a distinct function within the ecosystem:

* **PrimeFi:** Omnichain lending and borrowing
* **PrimeStaking:** Liquid staking and yield generation
* **PrimePort:** NFT marketplace infrastructure

Together, they form a **closed-loop system** where:

* Fees
* Liquidity
* Rewards

flow between protocols and back to **$PRFI holders**.
