# Ecosystem Benefits

The Prime Numbers ecosystem is designed so that **participation in any single protocol benefits holders across the entire system**.

***

### <mark style="color:purple;">Higher Yield & Rewards</mark>

Every protocol within the ecosystem generates fees.

* A defined share of those fees flows directly to **PRFI NFT holders**
* Returns scale as the ecosystem grows
* Yield compounds across multiple revenue sources

***

### <mark style="color:purple;">Omnichain Access</mark>

Built on LayerZero, the ecosystem operates across:

* XDC
* Hyperliquid
* Base
* Ethereum

Users can:

* Move assets across chains
* Manage collateral seamlessly
* Access yield without wrapped tokens or centralized bridges

***

### <mark style="color:purple;">Liquid Positions</mark>

PrimeStaking introduces **psXDC**, a liquid derivative of staked XDC.

* Staking positions are held inside NFTs
* Positions can be:
  * Transferred
  * Traded on PrimePort
  * Used as collateral in PrimeFi

All while continuing to generate yield.

***

### <mark style="color:purple;">Security First</mark>

All core contracts undergo independent audits before mainnet deployment.

Prime Numbers works with:

* QuillAudits
* PeckShield
* Nethermind Security

Additionally:

* Active Immunefi bug bounty program
* Continuous monitoring and upgrades

→ See full audit list

***

### <mark style="color:purple;">DAO Governance (soon)</mark>

$PRFI holders participate in protocol governance.

They can vote on:

* Protocol parameters
* Fee structures
* New asset listings
* Treasury decisions

Voting power is proportional to the amount of **$PRFI held**.
