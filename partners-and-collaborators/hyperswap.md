# HyperSwap

### Overview

**HyperSwap** is the native decentralized exchange (DEX) of the **HyperEVM ecosystem**.

It provides on-chain liquidity and trading infrastructure for assets deployed on Hyperliquid.

***

### Integration with PrimeFi

PrimeFi has integrated HyperSwap to ensure **instant liquidity for $PRFI** from the moment of its Token Generation Event (TGE).

***

### What It Enables

$PRFI holders on HyperEVM can:

* Swap tokens directly on-chain
* Access liquidity pools
* Earn trading fee rewards
* Use AMM-based swaps without intermediaries

This removes the need to rely on centralized exchanges for liquidity.

***

### Role in the Ecosystem

HyperSwap strengthens Prime Numbers by:

* Providing immediate liquidity for $PRFI
* Enabling decentralized trading on HyperEVM
* Supporting a fully on-chain user experience
