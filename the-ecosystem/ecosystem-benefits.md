# Ecosystem Benefits

The Prime Numbers ecosystem is designed so that participation in any one protocol\
benefits holders across the entire system.

#### Higher Yield & Rewards

Every protocol generates fees. A defined share of those fees flows directly to\
PRFI NFT holders, meaning your returns compound as the ecosystem grows.

#### Omnichain Access

Built on LayerZero, the ecosystem operates across XDC, Hyperliquid, Base, and\
Ethereum. Users can move assets, manage collateral, and access yield across chains\
without wrapped tokens or centralized bridges.

#### Liquid Positions

PrimeStaking introduces psXDC, a liquid derivative of staked XDC. Staking\
positions are held inside NFTs, which means they can be transferred, traded on\
PrimePort, or used as collateral in PrimeFi without losing yield.

#### Security First

Every core contract has been independently audited before mainnet deployment.\
Prime Numbers works with QuillAudits, PeckShield, and Nethermind Security,\
and maintains an active Immunefi bug bounty program.\
→ See full audit list

#### DAO Governance

$PRFI holders vote on protocol parameters, fee structures, new asset listings,\
and treasury decisions. The more $PRFI you hold, the more weight your vote carries.
