# XDC Staking NFTs Mechanics

## <mark style="color:purple;">Understanding XDC Staking NFTs: Maximizing Your Rewards</mark>

Our XDC Staking NFTs introduce a groundbreaking staking system within the Prime Numbers Labs ecosystem, allowing you to stake **psXDC** (liquid-staked XDC) inside NFTs to earn XDC rewards.

***

### <mark style="color:blue;">How It Works</mark>

1. **Staking psXDC:** By depositing psXDC inside your NFT, you increase its intrinsic value and unlock rewards from the XDC rewards pool. Your staked psXDC remains secure and can be withdrawn anytime unless locked.
2. **Leveling Up and Multipliers:** As you stake more psXDC, your NFT levels up, increasing its multiplier. The Total Multiplier\* (Base Multiplier + Added Multiplier) determines your share of the rewards pool. For example, an NFT with a Base Multiplier of 2.0 and a level 18 (Added Multiplier of 1.8) would have a Total Multiplier of 3.8.
3. **Rewards and Maximum Staking:** You can stake up to 100,000 psXDC inside each NFT. Once this limit is reached, any additional rewards will be added to your NFT each month. You can withdraw excess psXDC using the “Get Surplus” button.

{% hint style="info" %}
\*Total Multiplier = Base Multiplier + Added Multiplier
{% endhint %}

### <mark style="color:blue;">Interface Buttons and Their Functions</mark>

* **Stake:** Stake psXDC inside your NFT to begin earning rewards.
* **Get Surplus:** Withdraw any psXDC above 100,000 from your NFT after reaching the top level.
* **Withdraw psXDC:** Withdraw staked psXDC from your NFT without burning it.
* **Burn To Redeem:** Permanently destroy the NFT to redeem all staked psXDC.
* **Transfer:** Move your NFT between wallets.
* **Merge NFTs:** Combine two NFTs of the same rarity to create a higher rarity NFT.
* **Lock NFT:** Lock your NFT for 1 year for an additional 1.25% APY bonus (up to 6% total). Locked NFTs cannot use the Burn, Merge, or Withdraw features.
* **Claim XDC:** Claim your monthly XDC rewards.
* **Sell:** List or auction your NFT on the PrimePort marketplace.

### <mark style="color:blue;">Maximizing Rewards</mark>

As your NFT levels up and gains multipliers, your rewards increase. The staking system allows you to actively manage and optimize your digital assets, providing flexibility and potential for growth.
